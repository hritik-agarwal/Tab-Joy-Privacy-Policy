Tab Joy Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

Tab Joy is a browser extension that enhances your browsing experience by adding colorful borders and emoji titles to your browser tabs. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Tab Joy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We respect your privacy and ensure that your browsing experience remains private.

What We Store Locally

Tab Joy stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Domain names (e.g., "google.com", "github.com")
  • Color preferences for each domain
  • Emoji preferences for each domain
  • Extension settings (border width, enabled/disabled status)

Note: This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any external servers.

What We Don't Collect

Tab Joy does NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Browsing history or URLs
  • Page content or text
  • User activity (clicks, keystrokes, mouse movements)
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials

How We Use Your Data

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Remember your color preferences for different websites
  • Apply your chosen emojis to tab titles
  • Maintain your extension settings

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. All information remains on your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, it's protected by your browser's built-in security measures. We don't have access to your stored preferences.

Third-Party Services

Tab Joy does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Tab Joy's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
